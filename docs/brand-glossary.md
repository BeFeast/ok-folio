# OK Folio — Brand & Product Glossary

Use these terms consistently in UI copy, docs, and public-facing surfaces.

- **OK Folio** — the product name. "OK" is a hidden founder mark; "Folio" is the metaphor.
- **Piece** — a single visual object (photo, painting, illustration, scan, screenshot, or
  other visual). Use **Piece** in product UI. **Item** is a neutral internal/API term only.
- **Folio** — a curated group/collection of pieces inside OK Folio.
- **Gallery** — the main viewing surface and the first/default screen.
  - **Magazine** — default Gallery mode; editorial rhythm with featured pieces.
  - **Library** — regular grid for scanning and managing many pieces.
  - **Wall** — quiet, immersive, museum-wall style viewing.
- **Inbox** — the review workspace for new/unreviewed pieces. Actions: Keep, Dismiss,
  Add to Folio.
- **Streams** — backstage/admin area for incoming sources and connectors. Internally this
  may be called Ingest or Channels.
- **Add Piece** — the manual, curated import flow (title, source, author/artist, date, notes).
- **Favorite** — the heart state/icon.

## Words to avoid in public copy

scraper · downloader · hoarder · surveillance · "AI-powered" / "AI-first" · archive-first
utility · and the legacy names: PhotoPrismSight, OK Sight Ex, PicEx, extractor.

## Voice

Calm, refined, personal, editorial, quiet confidence. Beauty first; intelligence stays in
the background.
